## Sample Curriculum - Module 1 (Detailed)

### "Why Traditional Development is Broken"

**Duration**: 60 minutes

**Learning Objectives**:
- Understand the economics of traditional software development
- Recognize inefficiencies in current practices
- Be receptive to alternative approaches

**Outline**:

**Section 1: The $100K Engineer Who Writes 10 Lines/Day (15 min)**

> "Let's do some math that will terrify you."

- Average senior engineer salary: $150K/year
- Working days: ~240/year
- Cost per day: $625
- Actual coding time: ~3-4 hours/day (meetings, email, Slack, context switching)
- Lines of production code per day: 10-50 (generous estimate)
- **Cost per line of code: $12.50 to $62.50**

Interactive exercise:
- "Pull up your GitHub. Count lines changed last week. Divide salary by that."
- (Watch executives realize the horrible truth)

**Section 2: The Coordination Tax (20 min)**

> "It's not the coding. It's the coordination."

Brooks' Law visualized:
- 2 engineers: 1 communication path
- 5 engineers: 10 communication paths
- 10 engineers: 45 communication paths
- 50 engineers: 1,225 communication paths

Show real Slack screenshots:
- 47 messages to ship a one-line bug fix
- 6-person meeting to decide button color
- 3 days of code review for 20 lines

The Spotify model, Agile, Scrum... all attempts to solve coordination.
- "What if we eliminated coordination entirely?"

**Section 3: The Myth of the 10x Engineer (15 min)**

> "You don't need a 10x engineer. You need a 100x system."

- 10x engineers exist, but you can't hire 50 of them
- 10x engineers quit when working with 1x engineers
- 10x engineers cost $500K+ and have egos

Show alternative:
- 1x engineer with 10x system = 10x output
- 100 autonomous agents with 1 orchestrator = 1000x output
- **Cost: 90% less than traditional team**

**Section 4: The Real Problem (10 min)**

> "Software development is a solved problem. Management is not."

The bottleneck is never coding:
- Figuring out WHAT to build (Product)
- Figuring out HOW to build it (Architecture)
- Ensuring it's GOOD (Quality)
- Deploying it SAFELY (DevOps)

If you could automate all of that...
- (Transition to Module 2)

**Materials**:
- Slide deck (50 slides)
- ROI calculator spreadsheet
- GitHub analysis tool
- Communication overhead visualizer

**Instructor Notes**:
- Make it uncomfortable (they need to feel the pain)
- Use THEIR data (have them analyze their own teams)
- Don't pitch solution yet (just pain)
- Set up the "aha moment" for Module 2

## Guarantee Framework

### Why Guarantees Work

1. **De-Risk the Decision**
   - Executives are risk-averse
   - Guarantee makes it a no-brainer

2. **Prove Confidence**
   - "We're so confident, we'll refund if you don't succeed"
   - Shows you believe in your own system

3. **Marketing Weapon**
   - "100% Money-Back Guarantee" in all ads
   - Differentiates from competitors

### Tier 1 Guarantee

> **Ship One Production-Ready Product in 90 Days or Full Refund**

**Definition of "Production-Ready"**:
- Deployed to public URL
- At least 1 real user (can be internal)
- Passes all quality gates (tests, security, etc.)
- CEO approves it

**Refund Process**:
- Email proof of non-completion at day 90
- 30-minute exit interview (learn why it failed)
- Full refund within 7 business days

**Expected Claims**: < 5%
- Most executives will ship (you're helping them)
- Some will ship but not deploy (doesn't count, but close)
- Very few will completely fail AND ask for refund (embarrassing)

### Tier 2 Guarantee

> **3 Production Products in 6 Months OR 50% Dev Cost Reduction or Full Refund**

**Two Ways to Win**:
1. Ship 3 products (proves velocity)
2. OR reduce dev costs by 50% (proves ROI)

**Expected Claims**: < 3%

### Tier 3 Guarantee

> **10 Production Products in 1 Year AND 70% Cost Reduction or Full Refund**

**High Bar** (but they're paying $150K):
- Must ship 10 products AND reduce costs 70%
- If they hit one but not both, partial refund

**Expected Claims**: < 1%

**Q: What if I don't have a technical background?**
A: Perfect! This training is designed for business leaders, not engineers. We teach you how to MANAGE autonomous dev teams, not how to code. If you can use Slack, you can use ConnectSW.

**Q: How is this different from just using ChatGPT or Copilot?**
A: ChatGPT helps engineers write code faster. Copilot helps with autocomplete. ConnectSW replaces the entire development PROCESS: product management, architecture, coding, testing, deployment, and quality assurance. It's not a coding assistant; it's an autonomous development system.

**Q: What's the time commitment?**
A: 2-3 days for training, then 2-4 hours per week managing your autonomous team. You'll spend LESS time on software development than you do today, but you'll ship MORE.

**Q: Do I need to fire my dev team?**
A: No! Most of our customers keep their senior engineers for strategy, architecture, and domain expertise. The agents handle execution. Think of it as augmentation, not replacement. (Though some customers do reduce headcount through attrition.)

**Q: What if I'm non-technical? Can I really manage a dev team?**
A: That's the point! You don't manage developers; you manage OUTCOMES. Tell the Orchestrator what you want ("Build a mobile app for X"), and it handles the rest. You just approve checkpoints. No coding knowledge required.

**Q: Is my data safe? Do you see my code?**
A: Your code stays in your Git repos. We orchestrate the process, but you control where code is written and stored. Enterprise plans include on-premise deployment options. All data encrypted in transit and at rest. SOC 2 compliant.

**Q: What if the training doesn't work for me?**
A: 100% money-back guarantee. If you don't ship a production-ready product within 90 days, we refund the full training cost. No questions asked.

**Q: Can I send my CTO instead of attending myself?**
A: You can, but we recommend YOU attend. This is a business transformation, not just a tech upgrade. CEOs who attend see 3x better results because they understand how to leverage the system strategically.

**Q: What technologies does ConnectSW support?**
A: All major stacks: React, Vue, Angular, Node, Python, Go, Java, .NET, Ruby, PHP, Rust. Any database: PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, etc. Any cloud: AWS, GCP, Azure, or on-premise. If a human can build it, agents can build it.

A: Most executives ship their first product within 30 days and see cost savings within 90 days. Full ROI (training cost recovered) typically happens within 6 months.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: Yes. For Tier 2 and Tier 3, we offer quarterly payment plans. Tier 1 is due upfront (we've found this correlates with commitment and success).

**Q: What's included in the license?**
A: Tier 1 includes 6-month Pro license ($174 value). Tier 2 includes 1-year Business license ($1,188+ value). Tier 3 includes 2-year Enterprise license ($240K+ value). All licenses can be extended at standard pricing.
