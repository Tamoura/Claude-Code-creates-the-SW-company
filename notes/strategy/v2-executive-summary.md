## v2.0 Strategic Pillars

### 1. Multi-Tenant Platform
- **What**: Shared infrastructure serving 10,000+ customers
- **Why**: 100x more efficient, enables freemium, faster growth
- **Impact**: Gross margins increase from 70% to 85%

### 2. Intelligence Layer
- **What**: Learn from millions of task executions across all customers
- **Why**: Each customer benefits from collective intelligence
- **Impact**: Task success rate improves from 85% to 95%, 30% faster execution

### 3. Agent Marketplace
- **What**: Create, share, monetize custom agents (blockchain, mobile, ML, etc.)
- **Why**: Ecosystem innovation, new revenue stream (30% take rate)
- **Impact**: $5M marketplace GMV by Year 4

### 4. Product Template Marketplace
- **What**: SaaS boilerplates, industry-specific templates, architecture patterns
- **Why**: Reduce time-to-first-product from days to hours
- **Impact**: 60% activation rate (vs 30% without templates)

### 5. Command Center (Web UI + Mobile)
- **What**: Real-time dashboard, agent monitor, checkpoint approval
- **Why**: Accessible to non-technical stakeholders (CEOs, PMs)
- **Impact**: 3x larger addressable market

### 6. Integration Ecosystem
- **What**: Slack, Linear, Jira, GitHub, Vercel, 50+ integrations
- **Why**: Fit into existing workflows, reduce friction
- **Impact**: 40% faster adoption in enterprises

### 7. Advanced Analytics
- **What**: ROI tracking, velocity metrics, quality scores, benchmarking
- **Why**: Prove quantitative value to executives/boards
- **Impact**: Reduce churn from 5% to 3%, increase upsells 2x

### 8. Enterprise Features
- **What**: SSO, audit logs, SOC 2, white-label, dedicated infrastructure
- **Why**: Win $100K+ ARR contracts
- **Impact**: 50% of revenue from 5% of customers (enterprise)

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Product-Led Growth (80% of customers, 30% of revenue)

- SEO, community, referrals
- Target: Solo founders, indie hackers, small teams

### Phase 2: Training-Led (15% of customers, 20% of revenue)
- Executive masterclasses (existing v1.0 model)
- Convert to Business/Enterprise
- High-touch, high-ACV
- Target: CEOs, CTOs, CIOs

### Phase 3: Sales-Led (5% of customers, 50% of revenue)
- Enterprise outbound (SDR + AE team)
- POCs, security reviews, multi-year contracts
- White-label partners (agencies, consultancies)
- Target: Fortune 500, large tech, government
