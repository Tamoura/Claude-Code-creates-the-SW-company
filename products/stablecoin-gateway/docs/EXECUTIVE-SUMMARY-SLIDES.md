## Slide 12: Risk Mitigation

### Critical Risks & Contingencies

**Risk 1: Stripe drops to 0.5%**
- **Impact**: Eliminates fee advantage
- **Mitigation**: Differentiate on non-custodial, launch subscription billing first
- **Contingency**: Pivot to B2B white-label

**Risk 2: Regulatory Crackdown**
- **Impact**: SEC classifies stablecoins as securities
- **Mitigation**: Geo-fence US, expand EU/LATAM, partner with licensed entities
- **Contingency**: Add KYC/AML (expensive but enables compliance)

**Risk 3: Low Wallet Adoption**
- **Impact**: Customers don't have wallets, conversion suffers
- **Mitigation**: Partner with on-ramps (Moonpay, Ramp), add "Buy USDC" button
- **Contingency**: Target merchants with crypto customers only

**Risk 4: Payment Success <90%**
- **Impact**: Blockchain congestion, gas spikes cause failures
- **Mitigation**: Default to Polygon (low fees), add Layer 2s for redundancy
- **Contingency**: Subsidize gas for first 10 payments per merchant
